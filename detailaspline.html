Aspirin

Description

Aspirin is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) that reduces fever, pain, and inflammation. It is commonly used for headaches, muscle pain, and to relieve the pain associated with conditions like arthritis.

Price: $3.50

Available Stock: 200 units

Dosage: 81 mg

Ingredients

  • Aspirin
  • Inactive Ingredients: Corn Starch, Magnesium Stearate, Hypromellose

Side Effects

  • Stomach irritation
  • Rash
  • Heartburn
  • Increased risk of bleeding

Precautions

Consult your doctor before using this medication if you have a history of stomach ulcers, gastrointestinal bleeding, or are on blood thinners.
