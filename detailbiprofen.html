Ibuprofen

Description

Ibuprofen is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) commonly used for reducing inflammation, pain, and fever. It is used for conditions like arthritis, muscle pain, and mild-to-moderate pain from various causes.

Price: $8.00

Available Stock: 100 units

Dosage: 200 mg

Ingredients

  • Ibuprofen
  • Inactive Ingredients: Microcrystalline Cellulose, Croscarmellose Sodium, Magnesium Stearate

Side Effects

  • Stomach upset
  • Rash
  • Headache
  • Increased risk of heart attack or stroke (with long-term use)

Precautions

Consult your doctor before using this medication if you have a history of stomach ulcers, high blood pressure, or heart disease.
