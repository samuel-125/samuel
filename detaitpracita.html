Paracetamol

Description

Paracetamol is commonly used for pain relief and reducing fever. It is a well-known over-the-counter medication that can be used for minor aches, headaches, arthritis, back pain, and muscle soreness.

Price: $5.00

Available Stock: 50 units

Dosage: 500 mg

Ingredients

  • Paracetamol (Acetaminophen)
  • Inactive Ingredients: Starch, Microcrystalline Cellulose, Magnesium Stearate

Side Effects

  • Allergic Reactions (e.g., rash, swelling)
  • Long-term use may harm the liver
  • Overuse can lead to kidney problems

Precautions

Consult your doctor before using this medication if you have a history of liver disease or are on other medications that affect the liver.
